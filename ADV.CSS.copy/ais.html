AI You Can Use

Chat Gpt

Est. November 2022

GPT is an AI language model developed by OpenAI that generates human-like text based on input prompts. It uses deep learning techniques, specifically a transformer architecture, to understand and generate natural language. Trained on vast amounts of text data, GPT is capable of answering questions, completing tasks, and engaging in conversations on a wide variety of topics. Its ability to maintain context over longer interactions makes it particularly useful for applications like chatbots, virtual assistants, and content creation. GPT's strength lies in its flexibility and adaptability, as it can perform many language-related tasks with minimal specific training. However, it also has limitations, such as occasionally producing inaccurate information or failing with complex reasoning tasks.

Grammarly

Created by Max Lyvtyn

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to help users improve their writing by providing real-time feedback on grammar, punctuation, spelling, and style. It offers suggestions to enhance clarity, engagement, and tone, making it suitable for both casual and professional writing. Grammarly also detects potential issues such as wordiness, passive voice, and overused phrases, allowing users to fine-tune their writing. Beyond basic corrections, it has features that assess writing goals, such as formality level and intent, tailoring feedback accordingly. Its browser extension and app integrations make it easy to use across platforms like Microsoft Word, Google Docs, and email. Grammarly is a valuable tool for students, professionals, and anyone looking to produce polished, effective writing.
