TOP THREE

StarWars: Jedi Survivor

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor follows Cal Kestis, a Jedi Knight struggling to survive against the ever-expanding power of the Empire. Set five years after the events of Jedi: Fallen Order, Cal embarks on a dangerous journey across the galaxy, searching for a safe haven while uncovering hidden threats and lost Jedi secrets. As he battles powerful foes, including deadly Inquisitors and new adversaries, he must rely on his evolving combat skills, Force abilities, and the support of his allies. With a gripping narrative, immersive worlds, and enhanced gameplay mechanics, Jedi: Survivor delivers a thrilling and emotional continuation of Cal’s fight for survival in a galaxy consumed by darkness.

Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V follows the intertwined lives of three criminals—Michael, a retired bank robber struggling with family issues; Franklin, a street-savvy hustler looking for bigger opportunities; and Trevor, a volatile and unpredictable outlaw. Set in the sprawling city of Los Santos, the trio gets caught in a web of crime, corruption, and betrayal as they take on high-stakes heists while dealing with law enforcement, rival gangs, and personal conflicts. With an expansive open world, dynamic missions, and an engaging storyline, GTA V offers players the freedom to explore, wreak havoc, and experience the criminal underworld from multiple perspectives in one of the most ambitious and immersive games ever created.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is a futuristic open-world RPG set in the dystopian metropolis of Night City, where advanced technology and corporate greed shape everyday life. Players take on the role of V, a mercenary outlaw searching for a cybernetic implant that holds the key to immortality. As they navigate the city's seedy underworld, V must make crucial choices that affect the story, build their skills with cybernetic enhancements, and form alliances with powerful factions, including the enigmatic rocker Johnny Silverhand, played by Keanu Reeves. With its deep narrative, immersive world-building, and dynamic combat system, Cyberpunk 2077 delivers a gripping experience of rebellion, ambition, and survival in a high-tech, low-life society.
